About Bake Mo, Kwenta Ko

Ang Masarap na Pastries na Hahanapin Mo

Our Story

Bake Mo, Kwenta Ko is a unique culinary-meets-commerce venture established by the dedicated students of 9-BK Group #4. Born out of the intersection between a passion for artisanal baking and the precision of financial bookkeeping, our brand represents the perfect "balance" of flavors and figures. We believe that a great bakery isn't just about the warmth of the oven, but the integrity of the kitchen and the accuracy of the ledger.

The "9-BK" Quality Standard

As students of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines Laboratory High School, we apply the same rigor to our dough as we do to our T-accounts. We don't just sell pastries; we provide a "Sweet Asset" to your day. Every product we serve undergoes a "strict audit" of taste and quality. Whether it’s our flaky croissants, savory pies, or signature breads, we ensure that the "bottom line" is always customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

We aim to prove that the technical world of accounting and the creative world of baking can blend into one delicious experience. We keep our records clean and our ovens hot, serving pastries that aren't just a "debit" to your wallet, but a major "credit" to your happiness. Ito ang lasang hahanapin-hanapin mo!
